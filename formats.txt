  SUPPORTED FORMATS

Standard Formats
================
Plain Text
PostScript
PDF

Image Formats
=============
TIFF format, usually files with a suffix '.tif'
GIF format, usually files with a suffix '.gif'
BMP format, usually files with a suffix '.bmp'
PCX format, usually files with a suffix '.pcx'

Image format files will be scaled to fit on a single page, preserving the
aspect ratio.
